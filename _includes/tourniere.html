Hier registrieren: GrubenGold Cup 2022

After the Corona break, SC AufRuhr will again host the Grubengoldcup Badminton on the first weekend in February from 04.02.2022 to 06.02.2022. This time you have the possibility to secure a starting place without obligation, there is a pre-registration! A binding decision can be made after the registration has been completed at the end of December 2021. You can change your data at any time with your chosen password or cancel your registration.
